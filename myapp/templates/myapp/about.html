About Us


MediKart is a highly regarded pharmacy chain in India. It is the largest and first-ever Omni-Channel Pharmacy network in Asia, with a vast network of over 5500 outlets strategically located in key locations across the country. The delivery service covers over 19000+ pin codes, making it highly accessible to people throughout India.



Mission


To make a positive difference in the care of people and the personal care organizations we serve. We provide ethical counsel and support to medical professionals and patients in order to enhance the safe use of medications and medical devices.
